4. Chapter 4 Enzyme Drugs¶

4.1. Basic teaching contents¶

  1. The concept and main production methods of enzyme drugs;

  2. The properties and applications of important enzyme drugs;

  3. Preparation methods of important enzyme drugs.

4.2. Key points¶

  1. Cautions in the process of enzyme separation and purification;

  2. The properties and applications of important enzyme drugs;

  3. The main production process of superoxide dismutase and its critical control points.

4.3. Difficult points¶

  1. The main production process of superoxide dismutase and its critical control points.

4.4. Main learning requirements¶

  1. Master the cautions in the separation and purification of enzyme drugs;

  2. Be familiar with the properties and applications of important enzyme drugs;

  3. Master the structure, properties, main production processes and critical control points of superoxide dismutase.
