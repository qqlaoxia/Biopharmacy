Chapter 4 Enzyme Drugs=====================================================================


Basic teaching contents-----------------------------------

1.	The concept and main production methods of enzyme drugs;2.	The properties and applications of important enzyme drugs;3.	Preparation methods of important enzyme drugs.
Key points----------------------

1.	Cautions in the process of enzyme separation and purification;2.	The properties and applications of important enzyme drugs;3.	The main production process of superoxide dismutase and its critical control points.
Difficult points----------------------------

1.	The main production process of superoxide dismutase and its critical control points.
Main learning requirements--------------------------------------

1.	Master the cautions in the separation and purification of enzyme drugs;2.	Be familiar with the properties and applications of important enzyme drugs;3.	Master the structure, properties, main production processes and critical control points of superoxide dismutase.
